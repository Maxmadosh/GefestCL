GefestScan Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

GefestScan is designed to scan, edit, print, and share PDF documents on iPhone and iPad. We respect your privacy and keep the app simple: GefestScan does not require user accounts and does not collect personal data for advertising or tracking.

Data Collection

GefestScan does not collect, sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties.

Documents and Images

Documents, scans, signatures, and stamps are processed locally on your device. The app does not upload your documents, photos, signatures, or stamps to our servers.

Camera and Photos

GefestScan may request access to the camera to scan documents and may allow you to select images or files for import. These permissions are used only for the features you choose to use inside the app.

Files and Sharing

When you share, print, or export a PDF, the action is handled through iOS system sharing and printing features. The destination is selected by you.

Analytics and Tracking

GefestScan does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or cross-app tracking.

Children's Privacy

GefestScan is a productivity tool and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy when the app changes. The updated version will be posted on this page.

Contact
